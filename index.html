Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

How soon can construction start?
Construction can commence as soon as all necessary permits and approvals are secured, typically within a few weeks to a couple of months after finalizing the planning and design phase. We prioritize efficiency while ensuring compliance with regulations to expedite the start of construction without compromising quality.
Do you have site supervisors and subcontractors?
Absolutely, we have experienced site supervisors who oversee all aspects of the construction process, ensuring that work is carried out efficiently, safely, and to the highest standards. Additionally, we collaborate with trusted subcontractors who specialize in various trades, allowing us to deliver comprehensive solutions while maintaining rigorous quality control throughout the project.
Do you have a project timeline?
Yes, we develop detailed project timelines for all our construction projects. These timelines outline key milestones and activities from start to finish, allowing us to effectively manage the project schedule, coordinate resources, and track progress. Our commitment to transparency means that clients have full visibility into the timeline, enabling them to stay informed and confident in the progress of their project.
Do you provide guarantees?
Yes, we stand behind the quality of our workmanship and materials. We offer guarantees on our construction projects to provide peace of mind to our clients. The specific guarantees depend on the nature of the project and will be detailed in the contract agreement. Our goal is to ensure that our clients are completely satisfied with the final result, and we are committed to addressing any issues that may arise during or after construction.
Does our project fit your schedule and business model?
Absolutely, we prioritize projects that align with our schedule and business model to ensure we can deliver the highest quality results within agreed timelines. We carefully assess each project to ensure it fits within our capacity and expertise, allowing us to provide the attention to detail and personalized service that our clients expect. Our goal is to meet and exceed your expectations while maintaining the integrity of our commitments to all our clients.
What other projects have you worked on?

Are you insured and certified?
Yes, we are fully insured and certified to undertake construction projects. Our company carries comprehensive insurance coverage, including general liability insurance and workers' compensation insurance, to protect both our clients and our team members throughout the construction process. Additionally, we hold all necessary certifications and licenses required to operate legally and ethically in the construction industry. Our commitment to compliance and safety ensures that our clients can have confidence in our capabilities and professionalism.
What is your payment schedule?
Our payment schedule is typically structured to align with project milestones and progress. Depending on the scope and duration of the project, payments may be divided into several installments, with an initial deposit required to secure our services. Subsequent payments are then made at key stages of the project, such as upon completion of specific phases or milestones, as outlined in the contract agreement. We aim to provide flexibility and transparency in our payment schedule, ensuring that it is fair and manageable for our clients while also reflecting the progress and quality of work delivered.
How often do projects miss deadlines?
At Leanotums Projects, we prioritize meticulous planning, effective communication, and proactive project management to minimize the likelihood of missing deadlines. While we strive to deliver projects on time, unforeseen circumstances such as weather delays, supply chain disruptions, or unexpected site conditions can occasionally impact our timelines. However, we mitigate these risks by implementing robust contingency plans, closely monitoring progress, and maintaining open communication with our clients throughout the project. Our track record demonstrates our commitment to meeting deadlines and delivering projects efficiently and effectively.
Is your bid an estimate or a fixed price?
Our bid typically represents a fixed price for the project, providing clarity and certainty to our clients regarding the total cost of construction. We understand the importance of budgetary transparency and strive to provide accurate and comprehensive quotes that encompass all aspects of the project, including materials, labor, permits, and overhead expenses. While unforeseen circumstances may arise during the course of the project, we are committed to honoring our fixed-price agreements to the best of our ability, ensuring that our clients can proceed with confidence and peace of mind.
